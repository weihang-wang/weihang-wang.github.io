About me

I am an Assistant Professor at University at Buffalo (SUNY Buffalo). My research interests are in software engineering, computer systems and software security.

Before joining University at Buffalo, I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Purdue University in 2018. I was very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Xiangyu Zhang and Prof. Patrick Eugster. I was awarded a Facebook Research Award in 2019, a Mozilla Research Award in 2019, and the Maurice H. Halstead Memorial Research Award (from Purdue University) in 2018.

I am looking for motivated Ph.D. students, undergraduate researchers, and visitors who are interested in software testing and software security. Please send me email if you are interested in working in my group, and attach a CV to your email.
