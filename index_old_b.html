ABOUT

I am a PhD candidate at Purdue University, working with Prof. Xiangyu Zhang and Prof. Patrick Eugster. Prior to joining Purdue, I received my master and bachelor degrees in Computer Science from Chinese Academy of Sciences and China Agricultural University respectively.
My research interests include static and dynamic analysis on web technologies. In particular, I am interested in detecting and preventing concurrency bugs, unauthorized DOM manipulations, and exploits such as web-bots, malicious browser extensions, and ad-blockers to help developers protect the integrity of their contents.
